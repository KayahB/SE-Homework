Trials and Tribulations

Something else that should be taken into account when starting your project is how much yarn will be used for whatever you desire to make. I cannot, and I mean CANNOT express to someone how many times I've gone to work on something, and got super into it, even more than halfway maybe, and then... bam. All of a sudden I see that I'm done with this skein and now I've got to stop working. More often than not that's what I'll do but on the rare occasion that it's not 2am or I'm feeling social, I'll go out right then and there to see if JoAnn's didn't run out of my go-to colors. Definitely not my favorite (or most used) option, but sometimes a garment needs to be *~expedited~*.
